Our Process

How a Long-Distance Move Works

Long-distance moves need more planning and coordination than local jobs. Here is how we make sure nothing falls through the cracks.

Initial Consultation

We start with a detailed conversation about your move — destination, volume, timeline, access at both ends, and any specialty items.

In-Home or Virtual Survey

We assess the full scope of your household to give you an accurate, binding quote. No surprises on moving day.

Packing & Loading

Our crew packs (if needed), wraps, and loads everything onto your dedicated truck. An inventory is created and shared with you.

Transit & Delivery

Your belongings are transported directly to your new home. We coordinate arrival timing and place everything exactly where you want it.

Why Choose Elite

What Sets Our Long-Distance Service Apart

  • Dedicated truck, not shared freight. Your belongings are not mixed with other households or transferred between vehicles.
